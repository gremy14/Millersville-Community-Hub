Millersville Non-Profits

In Millersville, PA there are many different organizations, but among those organizations there are many non-profit organizations and organizations whose overall goal is to help out those in need. What makes non-profits amazing is they do all this for free, and no anticipating to be paid. These wonderful and kind workers are running off the kindness in their heart and to help those in need.

Loft Community Partnership- Community Center

The loft is a non-profti organization that collects food, clothes, and all the essentials for those in need. It is ran by individuals who volunter and dedicate thier time to help all of those people that need the help. The loft also brings the community together and make a vibe that includes in everyone, no matter from where, to help their community out.

Lancaster Lebanon Habitat for Humanity

The Lancaster Lebanon Habitat for Humanity is a Christian ran non-profit. Throughout almost 40 years of exsiting their vision has never changed, giving everyone a decent place to live. This organization's main job is building homes. This makes them significantly different than most non-profits since most non-profits is helping people and collecting food, the difference is dramatic since building homes for people in need is a lot of work. This organization dedicates their time, energy, and money, to help those in need of a home.
