Along with providing numerous schooling opportunities, Penn Manor Schol District povides its students with various sports. One of the most notable sports teams that Penn Manor has is its Field Hockey team. Penn Manor Field Hockey is ranked 42nd in the nation, according to MaxReps. Penn Manor Field Hockey has also produced two Olympic competitors who were on the 2024 Paris Olympic team. Another impressive achievement for Penn Manor Field Hockey is almost making it to the sate Finals in their 2025 season. Not just Penn Manor's Field Hockey team is good, their wrestling team has some outstanding wrestlers. Some notable wrestlers are Caden Boland, Gavin Boland, and Travis Clawson. All three of these wrestlers went to the State Championships and placed in the top 8 for their weight class.
