Millersville University's ComMUnity Fest

ComMUnity Fest is Millersville's signature event connecting Millersville University's campus with local businesses, non-profits, and community organizations.

Millersville's Annual Turkey Trot

Milersvillle has an annual turkey trot 5K the day that Thanksgiving is on. This event brings together everyone in the community, even if you don't run and just walk it you're still welcomed!
