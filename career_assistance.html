Support Millersville University Provides

  • eCritique Services

    Millersville Univeristy provides an online career help program. The program is called Electronic Career Document Reviews. In this program you will send in your documents, like resume, and then professors and helpers at MIllersville will review your document and critique it. This program helps students bolster their chance of getting a job by brushing up their resume and other documents to make them look more professional.
