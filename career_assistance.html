Support Millersville University Provides

  • eCritique Services

    Millersville Univeristy provides an online career help program. The program is called Electronic Career Document Reviews. In this program you will send in your documents, like resume, and then professors and helpers at MIllersville will review your document and critique it. This program helps students bolster their chance of getting a job by brushing up their resume and other documents to make them look more professional.

  • Career Exploration

    Millersville's Career Center provides you with the FOCUS-2 Career Assessment. This assessment is used to help you explore who you are and how your skills, interests, and strengths will contribute to your field of study. Throught this assessment students learn where their strengths and weaknesses are and through this will decide on their Major. After this most students will meet with their Career COunselor and develop a 4-year college plan on classes to take for their degree.

  • Employment Opportunities

    Millersville University offers a recruiting platform for students and graduates to connect, this is called Handshake. Handshake provides current students with Internships, full-time job opportunities, along with part-time and volunteer opportunities through many organizations.

  • Career Shift

    Career Shift is an employment and internship search. In it you can select from hundreds of companies to narrow your field you're searching for down. The internships that are offered range from US to Canada to Uk and Austalia.
