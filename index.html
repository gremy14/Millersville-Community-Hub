The Millersville Community Hub

The Millersville Community Hub is a website that is designed to bring together the community and be a helpful resource tool. In this Hub you can find many different topics ranging from Non-profits, to Career Assistance programs. Our purpose is to help you find the resources that you need in the Millersville Community.
